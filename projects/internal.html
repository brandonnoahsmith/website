brandon smith

internal
2014

internal is a spatial exploration produced at the washington university in st. louis pre-college summer discovery program
the work is highly fluid and transversed between multiple mediums
 these cubes were an exploration in how line transverses across differing planes and a juxtaposition of a contorted interior with a flat exterior
they attempt to create a conceptual space, with the internal protrusions allowing for the presence of light and formulating partial walls the wooden model depicts a landscape that creates natural living spaces
the design and layout were the three-dimensional derivation of a series of two-dimensional drawings, ultimately originating from hexagonal geometry
